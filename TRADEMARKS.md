A third party may use the word mark solely to identify SurveySentinel products or compatibility, provided that the use is accurate, not misleading, not more prominent than the third party's own branding and does not suggest sponsorship, certification or endorsement.

- use a SurveySentinel mark in a company, product, service, domain or social-media name;
- reproduce or alter a SurveySentinel logo;
- imitate SurveySentinel trade dress or visual identity;
