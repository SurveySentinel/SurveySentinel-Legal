## Governance objective

SurveySentinel legal and compliance documents are controlled records. They must be accurate, internally consistent, assigned to an owner and reviewed when products, processing activities, laws, markets or business arrangements materially change.

## Roles

| Role | Responsibility |
|---|---|
| Document owner | Maintains accuracy and initiates review |
| Technical owner | Confirms product and engineering statements |
| Privacy lead | Reviews personal-data processing and notices |
| Security lead | Reviews security commitments and disclosure processes |
| Commercial owner | Confirms product, pricing and customer applicability |
| Legal approver | Approves legal effect and risk allocation |

One person may hold several roles in an early-stage business, but approval decisions must still be documented.

## Approval gates

Legal review is required before:

- first commercial release;
- entering a new jurisdiction or regulated market;
- introducing cloud processing, telemetry or customer uploads;
- materially changing licence enforcement, AI functions or data sharing;
- adding high-risk third-party licences; or
- changing liability, warranty, indemnity, governing-law or dispute provisions.

## Review cycle

Documents must be reviewed at least annually and sooner after a material change. Superseded versions must be retained in version control and clearly marked as archived.

## Exceptions

Exceptions require a written risk decision stating scope, rationale, owner, controls and expiry date.
