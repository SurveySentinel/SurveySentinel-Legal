## Before commercial launch

The following actions are mandatory:

1. insert the full supplier legal entity, company number, registered office and contact details;
2. obtain UK solicitor review of the EULA, Terms of Service, Privacy Notice, liability terms and consumer applicability;
3. confirm actual product data flows, telemetry, cloud processing, subprocessors and retention periods;
4. generate the third-party register from the released software bill of materials;
5. confirm aviation, insurance, export-control and sanctions obligations for target markets; and
6. change a document to **Approved** only through the process in `DOCUMENT_CONTROL.md`.

## Interpretation

Start with [`LEGAL_OVERVIEW.md`](LEGAL_OVERVIEW.md). Where a signed customer agreement conflicts with a general repository document, the signed agreement normally takes priority as described in the Legal Framework Overview.
