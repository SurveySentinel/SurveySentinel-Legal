## Purpose

This repository contains the public legal, licensing, governance and compliance framework for the SurveySentinel software platform. The documents are intended to be read together and may be supplemented or replaced by product-specific terms, an order form, a data processing agreement or a separately signed customer agreement.

## Document groups

- **Intellectual property:** `LICENSE`, `NOTICE.md`, `COPYRIGHT.md` and `TRADEMARKS.md`.
- **Product use:** `EULA.md`, `DISCLAIMER.md` and `TERMS_OF_SERVICE.md`.
- **Privacy and security:** `PRIVACY_POLICY.md`, `DATA_RETENTION_POLICY.md`, `SECURITY.md` and `VULNERABILITY_DISCLOSURE.md`.
- **Technology governance:** `OPEN_SOURCE_POLICY.md`, `THIRD_PARTY_LICENSES.md` and `AI_USAGE_POLICY.md`.
- **Regulatory governance:** `EXPORT_CONTROL.md`, `GOVERNANCE.md` and `DOCUMENT_CONTROL.md`.

## Priority of terms

Where documents conflict, the following order of priority applies unless a signed agreement states otherwise:

1. a signed customer agreement or order form;
2. product-specific licence terms;
3. the End User Licence Agreement;
4. the Website and Online Services Terms;
5. this repository's general policies and notices.

## Legal entity details required before release

Before commercial launch, replace all bracketed placeholders with the legal name, registered number, registered office, trading address, privacy contact and security contact of the entity that owns and supplies SurveySentinel.
